About

Pakistan Agricultural Research Council

(PARC) is a premier institution dedicated to advancing agricultural research and development across Pakistan. Established with the mission to improve the livelihoods of farmers and ensure food security, PARC leads in exploring sustainable agricultural practices, innovative farming technologies, and research programs that benefit Pakistan's diverse agricultural sector.

ALP

The Agricultural Linkage Program (ALP) supports research initiatives aimed at enhancing productivity and sustainability in Pakistan's agriculture sector.

PIASA

Pakistan Institute of Advanced Studies in Agriculture (PIASA) provides advanced education and training for agricultural professionals in the latest research and technologies.

KISAN CORNER

Kisan Corner offers resources and support for farmers, helping them access the latest information on crops, techniques, and market opportunities.

PJAR

The Pakistan Journal of Agricultural Research (PJAR) publishes cutting-edge research findings to advance agricultural practices and innovations in Pakistan.

PATCO

Pakistan Agricultural Technology Company (PATCO) bridges the gap between research and industry by commercializing agricultural technologies and innovations.

PATCO PORTAL

The PATCO Portal provides an online platform for accessing technology-driven solutions, products, and services for the agricultural community.
